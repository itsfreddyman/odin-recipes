Best Ramen Noodles

Description

Easy to cook.

Delicious.

Hope you guys will enjoy.

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups of water
  2. 1 cup cubed cooked chicken
  3. teaspoon dried minced onion
  4. 4 pinches Creole seasoning
  5. 1 dash hot sauce
  6. 1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles with seasoning packet
  7. 1 slice pepper Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Pour water into a mideum saucepan.

    Add chicken, onion,Creole seasoning, and hot sauce.

    Bring mixture to a rolling boil.

    Add noodles and reduce heat to the medium; cool for 3 minutes.

  2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the including seasoning pack and pepper Jack cheese.

    Let cool slightly before serving.
