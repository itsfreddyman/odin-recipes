Delicious Pancakes

Description

Best recipes for the early breakfast

Easy to make and Delicious

Hope you gus will enjoy

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. 2 tablespoons white sugar
  3. 2 tablespoons baking powder
  4. 1 tablespoon salt
  5. 1 egg, beaten
  6. 1 cup milk
  7. 2 tablespoons vegatable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.

    Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil.

    Mix until smooth.

  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.

    Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

    Brown on both sides and serve hot.
